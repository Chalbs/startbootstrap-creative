Frequently Asked Questions

What is ZenFit?

ZenFit is our featured product at SkillMob. It is a daily program to help you learn to meditate, practice mindfulnes, and improve your congitive funtion.

How does it work?

Each day you'll receive a 15 minute guided program. This program helps you focus inward and calm your mind. You'll be guided through breathing exercises, thought exercises, and focused meditation. At the end of any session you'll be asked a question that you can ponder and write in our journal.

Why ZenFit?

ZenFit is designed to help you manage stress and operate at peak performance. At SkillMob, we're all about improving our skills and learning new things so we can be our best. This starts with a strong mind, which there is no better way to prepare your mind with meditation and mindfulness practice.

What if it doesn't work for me?

We'll give you a refund, all your money back in your pocket. We offer a 30 day money back promise if you're not enjoying our programming and experiencing a positive result in your life. However, we want to make sure you give it everything you got before throwing in the towel. :-)
